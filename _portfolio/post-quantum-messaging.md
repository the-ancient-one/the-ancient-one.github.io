### Highlights
- Implemented post‑quantum cryptographic primitives using **Kyber (KEM)** and **Dilithium (signatures)**.
- Focused on practical secure messaging architecture and threat modelling.
- Designed for real-world operational constraints rather than purely theoretical evaluation.
